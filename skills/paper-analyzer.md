# Research Paper Analyzer Skill

## Objective

Extract key insights from academic or technical research papers.

Focus on methodology, contributions, and practical implications.

---

# Workflow

## Step 1 — Identify Paper Context

Determine:

- research problem
- domain
- authors and institution
- publication venue

---

## Step 2 — Extract Core Contributions

Identify:

- main innovation
- proposed methodology
- theoretical contributions

---

## Step 3 — Analyze Methodology

Understand:

- model architecture
- algorithms used
- datasets
- experimental setup

---

## Step 4 — Evaluate Results

Look for:

- benchmarks
- performance improvements
- comparisons to prior work

---

## Step 5 — Identify Limitations

Determine:

- assumptions
- weaknesses
- open problems

---

# Output Format

Paper Analysis

Research Problem

Key Contributions

Methodology

Results

Limitations

Practical Implications

Sources
