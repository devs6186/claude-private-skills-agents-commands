# Research Synthesizer Skill

## Objective

Combine research findings from multiple sources into a coherent analysis.

Focus on:

- synthesis of insights
- identifying contradictions
- extracting actionable conclusions.

---

# Workflow

## Step 1 — Aggregate Research Inputs

Collect outputs from research skills such as:

- web-researcher
- github-researcher
- reddit-researcher
- twitter-researcher
- stackoverflow-researcher
- hackernews-researcher
- documentation-analyzer
- paper-analyzer
- ecosystem-scanner

---

## Step 2 — Identify Core Themes

Look for patterns across sources.

Identify:

- repeated insights
- consensus opinions
- dominant architectural ideas

---

## Step 3 — Detect Contradictions

Highlight disagreements between sources.

Examples:

- documentation claims vs developer complaints
- marketing claims vs real-world performance

Clearly label contradictions.

---

## Step 4 — Extract Key Insights

Identify:

- major technical lessons
- common pitfalls
- emerging trends
- strategic implications

---

## Step 5 — Produce Final Report

# Output Format

Research Summary

Executive Overview

Major Insights

Consensus Findings

Contradictions

Advantages

Limitations

Trend Analysis

Actionable Takeaways
