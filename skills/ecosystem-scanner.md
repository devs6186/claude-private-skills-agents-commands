# Ecosystem Scanner Skill

## Objective

Assess the health and growth of a technology ecosystem.

Identify whether a project is:

- growing rapidly
- stable and mature
- declining

---

# Workflow

## Step 1 — Identify Ecosystem Signals

Look for indicators such as:

- GitHub activity
- number of contributors
- integrations
- third-party tooling

---

## Step 2 — Analyze Industry Adoption

Check for:

- company usage
- production deployments
- startup adoption
- enterprise integrations

---

## Step 3 — Evaluate Community Activity

Assess:

- conference talks
- blog posts
- tutorials
- developer discussions

---

## Step 4 — Identify Ecosystem Strength

Determine:

- maturity level
- ecosystem size
- long-term sustainability

---
