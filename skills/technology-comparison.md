# Technology Comparison Skill

## Objective

Provide a structured comparison between multiple technologies.

Focus on engineering tradeoffs rather than marketing claims.

---

# Workflow

## Step 1 — Identify Technologies

Determine the technologies to compare.

Example:

- frameworks
- databases
- AI tools
- infrastructure platforms

---

## Step 2 — Collect Key Attributes

For each technology analyze:

- architecture design
- scalability characteristics
- ecosystem maturity
- learning curve
- community adoption

---

## Step 3 — Identify Strengths and Weaknesses

Evaluate:

- best use cases
- known limitations
- developer experience
- operational complexity

---

## Step 4 — Determine Decision Criteria

Identify factors influencing the choice:

- performance requirements
- scalability needs
- team expertise
- ecosystem support

---
