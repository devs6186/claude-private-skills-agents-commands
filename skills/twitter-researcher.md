# Twitter Intelligence Skill

## Objective

Extract insights from developer conversations on X (Twitter).

Focus on sentiment, trends, announcements, and practitioner experiences.

---

# Workflow

## Step 1 — Identify Relevant Keywords

Determine:

- project name
- framework name
- key hashtags
- developer accounts

---

## Step 2 — Scan Relevant Posts

Look for:

- developer threads
- product announcements
- expert commentary
- launch discussions
- critical feedback

Prioritize posts from:

- maintainers
- engineers
- researchers
- industry leaders

---

## Step 3 — Extract Insights

Identify:

- sentiment toward the technology
- major criticisms
- emerging use cases
- adoption patterns

---

## Step 4 — Detect Trends

Look for:

- sudden spikes in discussion
- major announcements
- new releases
- community excitement or backlash

---

# Output Format

Twitter Insights

Community Sentiment

Key Discussions

Notable Opinions

Emerging Trends

Sources
