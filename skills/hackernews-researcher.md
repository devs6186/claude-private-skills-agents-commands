# HackerNews Researcher Skill

## Objective

Analyze Hacker News discussions to extract high-signal engineering insights about technologies, frameworks, infrastructure tools, and startups.

Hacker News discussions often contain experienced engineers sharing practical insights, criticisms, and architecture considerations.

---

# Workflow

## Step 1 — Identify Relevant Discussions

Search Hacker News for:

- posts mentioning the technology or project
- launch announcements
- architecture discussions
- technical blog posts shared on HN

Prioritize threads with:

- high comment counts
- high upvotes
- detailed technical discussion

---

## Step 2 — Extract Engineering Opinions

Identify insights such as:

- architecture critiques
- scaling concerns
- design tradeoffs
- real-world experiences

Focus on technically detailed comments.

---

## Step 3 — Identify Patterns

Look for recurring themes:

- repeated praise
- common criticisms
- suggested alternatives
- industry context

---

## Step 4 — Filter Noise

Ignore:

- jokes or memes
- vague opinions without explanation
- comments lacking technical substance

---

# Output Format

HackerNews Insights

Key Discussions

Engineering Opinions

Common Criticisms

Suggested Alternatives

Industry Context

Sources
